# Marth ×5

## Stat Bonuses

- **Lv 1**：Str+1  Dex+1  Spd+1
- **Lv 5**：Str+1  Dex+2  Spd+2
- **Lv 10**：Str+2  Dex+3  Spd+3
- **Lv 20**：Str+3  Dex+4  Spd+4

## Sync Skills

- **Lv 1**：Break Defenses
  - If unit's attack breaks foe, unit
makes an extra attack at 50%
damage.
- **Lv 5**：Convoy
  - Use to deposit or retrieve weapons
and items.
- **Lv 10**：Shieldbreaker
  - Grants the unit "Shieldbreaker" command,
use 3 engage meter or 1 engage turn
to get Seal Defense and Seal Resistance 
for the next battle.
If the foe is defeated, apply the effect
to the enemies in 2 radius of the foe.
- **Lv 10**：Unyielding
  - If the unit is attacked, and their
HP is lower than 50% after battle,
grant +1 Mov, and when initiate
combat, Dmg+5. At the end of each
player's phase, if the unit has not
initiate any combat, lose the effect.
- **Lv 20**：Unyielding+
  - If the unit is attacked, and their
HP is lower than 70% after battle,
grant +1 Mov, and when initiate
combat, Dmg+7. At the end of each
player's phase, if the unit has not
initiate any combat, lose the effect.

## Engage Skills

- **Lv 1**：Divine Speed
  - Unit performs an extra attack
at 50% damage in combat.
- **Lv 1**：Divine Speed (Dragon)
  - Unit performs an extra attack
at 50% damage in combat.
[Dragon] Unit recovers HP equal to
damage dealt by extra attack.

## Engage Attack

- **Lodestar Rush**

- **Lodestar Rush (Mystical)**
  - Use to launch 7 consecutive sword
attacks at 30% damage. Adjacent foe
only.
[Mystical] Damage based on Mag.
- **Lodestar Rush (Dragon)**
  - Use to launch 7 consecutive sword
attacks at 30% damage. Adjacent foe
only.
[Dragon] +2 attacks.
- **Lodestar Rush (Backup)**
  - Use to launch 7 consecutive sword
attacks at 30% damage. Adjacent foe
only.
[Backup] +1 attack.
