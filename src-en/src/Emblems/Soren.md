## Sync Skills

- **Lv 1**：Anima Focus
  - Grants Atk+4 if unit attacks with:

wind, and foe's style is Flying.
- **Lv 1**：Tactical Advice
  - Grants the ability to use
multiple positioning commands.
- **Lv 5**：Keen Insight
  - When unit has weapon advantage,
grants Atk+3.
- **Lv 5**：Block Recovery
  - When foe uses a Revival Stone,
they recover only 50% of their HP.
- **Lv 10**：Block Recovery+
  - When foe uses a Revival Stone,
they recover only 20% of their HP.
- **Lv 20**：Keen Insight+
  - When unit has weapon advantage,
grants Atk+5.
- **Lv 20**：Block Recovery++
  - When foe uses a Revival Stone,
they recover only 1 HP.
