# Sigurd ×5

## Stat Bonuses

- **Lv 1**：Dex+1  Spd+1  Lck-2  Def+1  Mov+1
- **Lv 5**：Dex+2  Spd+1  Lck-2  Def+2  Mov+1
- **Lv 10**：Dex+3  Spd+1  Lck-2  Def+3  Mov+1
- **Lv 20**：Dex+4  Spd+2  Lck-2  Def+4  Mov+1

## Sync Skills

- **Lv 1**：Canter
  - Unit can move 2 spaces after acting.
- **Lv 5**：Momentum
  - Grants Atk+1 to first attack during
combat for each mov point unit cost
before attacking(max 9).
- **Lv 10**：Headlong Rush
  - Grants the unit "Rush" command,
can pass through the foe after battle.
- **Lv 10**：Anti-Mounted
  - When fighting against a mounted unit,
Hit+10, Dmg+2.
- **Lv 20**：Canter+
  - Unit can move 3 spaces after acting.
- **Lv 20**：Anti-Mounted+
  - When fighting against a mounted unit,
Hit+15, Dmg+3.
- **Lv 20**：Momentum+
  - Grants Atk+1 to first attack during
combat for each mov point unit cost
before attacking. If unit cost move
points>=9, the first attack would
break foe.
