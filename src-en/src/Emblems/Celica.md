## Sync Skills

- **Lv 1**：Echo
  - Grant this unit "Echo" command,
use to perform magical attack with
50% damage, but can attack twice.
- **Lv 1**：Echo (Mystical)
  - Grant this unit "Echo" command,
use to perform magical attack with
50% damage, but can attack twice.
[Mystical] Damage increase to 60%
- **Lv 1**：Echo (Dragon)
  - Grant this unit "Echo" command,
use to perform magical attack with
50% damage, but can attack twice.
[Dragon] Damage increase to 60%
- **Lv 1**：Resonance
  - When fighting with a tome, grants
Dmg+3. If the foe survives after battle,
the unit suffers Def/Res-1.
(The debuff lasts for 1 turn.)
- **Lv 5**：Counter
  - When an adjacent enemy initiate and
hit this unit, deals the same damage
taken by this unit to the foe.
- **Lv 10**：Countermagic
  - When hit by a magical attack,
deals the same damage taken by
the user to the foe.
- **Lv 10**：Resonance+
  - When fighting with a tome, grants
Dmg+5. If the foe survives after battle,
the unit suffers Def/Res-1.
(The debuff lasts for 1 turn.)
- **Lv 20**：Counter+
  - When an adjacent enemy hit 
this unit, deals the same damage
taken by this unit to the foe.
- **Lv 20**：CounterMagic+
  - When hit by magical attacks,
deals the same damage to the foe.

## Engage Skills

- **Lv 1**：Chaos Style
  - Increase damage dealt and restore
HP equals to the increased damage.
For physical attack, increase 20% of 
enemy's Mag. For magical attack,
increase 20% of enemy's Str.
