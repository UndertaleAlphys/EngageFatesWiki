# Edelgard ×6

## Stat Bonuses

- **Lv 1**：Str+1  Dex+1  Def+1
- **Lv 5**：Str+2  Dex+2  Def+2
- **Lv 10**：Str+3  Dex+3  Def+2
- **Lv 20**：Str+4  Dex+4  Def+3

## Sync Skills

- **Lv 1**：Friendly Rivalry
  - At start of player phase, the Emblem
will randomly switch.
If sycned emblem is:
Edelgard, grants immunity to break;
Dimitri, grants mov+1 and canto;
Claude, grants mov+1, and unit pays 
no extra cost on any terrain.
- **Lv 1**：Combat Arts
  - Enables use of Edelgard, Dimitri, or
Claude's Combat Art based on sycned emblem.
- **Lv 5**：Weapon Sync
  - Grants Atk +3 when synced with an Emblem 
wielding the same weapon type.
- **Lv 10**：Charm
  - If unit's attack triggers a chain
attack, increases chain attack
Dmg by 50%.
- **Lv 20**：Weapon Sync+
  - Grants Atk +5 when synced with an Emblem 
wielding the same weapon type.

## Engage Skills

- **Lv 1**：Limit Breaker
  - Grants unit all effects of Friendly Rivalry.
Enables use of Edelgard, Dimitri, or
Claude's Combat Art regardless of sycned emblem.
Triggers "Weapon Sync" regardless of
equipped weapons.

## Engage Attack

- **Houses Unite**
  - Use to attack with Aymr(Freeze), Areadbhar(Break),
and Failnaught(Silence) at 50% damage.
- **Houses Unite (Dragon)**
  - Use to attack with Aymr(Freeze), Areadbhar(Break),
and Failnaught(Silence) at 50% damage.
[Dragon] +10% damage.

## Engage Weapons

- **Lv 1**：Aymr: Range 1, Mt 36, Hit 60, Crit 20 / Axe of Emblem Edelgard. Smashes
foes. Cannot follow up, or strike first
if initiating combat. Eff: Dragon.
- **Lv 10**：Areadbhar: Range 1, Mt 19, Hit 75, Crit 10 / Lance wielded by Emblem Dimitri.
If user initiates combat, grants
Mt+20%.
- **Lv 20**：Failnaught: Range 2, Mt 18, Hit 75, Crit 20 / Bow wielded by Emblem Claude.
If user initiates combat, grants
Avo+15. Effective: Dragon, Flying.

- **Lv 1**：Defense +1

- **Lv 5**：Weapon Sync

wielding the same weapon type.

- **Lv 10**：Charm
  - Cost 1200 | If unit's attack triggers a chain

Dmg by 50%.

- **Lv 10**：Knightkneeler
  - Cost 2000 | Grant the unit "Knightkneeler".
Cost 4 engage meter or 2 engage turn

Effective: Cavalry.
- **Lv 20**：Weapon Sync+
  - Cost 2000 | Grants Atk +5 when synced with an Emblem 
wielding the same weapon type.

  - Cost 2000 | Grant the unit "Monster Crusher".
