# Camilla ×7

## Stat Bonuses

- **Lv 1**：Str+1  Spd+1  Def+1  Mov+1
- **Lv 5**：Str+1  Spd+1  Def+2  Mov+1
- **Lv 10**：Str+2  Spd+1  Def+2  Mov+1
- **Lv 20**：Str+3  Spd+2  Def+3  Mov+1

## Sync Skills

- **Lv 1**：Air Superiority
  - When fighting against a
flying unit, grants Hit/Avo+15.
- **Lv 1**：Lunge
  - Grants the unit "Lunge" command,
use to swap places with an enemy after battle.
- **Lv 5**：Savage Blow
  - If unit initiates combat, deals damage
to foes within 2 spaces equal to 20%
of their max HP after combat.
- **Lv 10**：Trample
  - Unless enemy is on a mount,
damage+5.
- **Lv 20**：Air Superiority+
  - When fighting against a
flying unit, grants Hit/Avo+30.

## Engage Skills

- **Lv 1**：Galeforce
  - When user triggers the battle, allows a 
full action if the user defeats the enemy
(once per Engage)

## Engage Attack
