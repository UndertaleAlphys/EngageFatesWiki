## Sync Skills

- **Lv 1**：Holy Aura
  - Unit is Effective: Fell Dragon. Allies
within 2 spaces with Effective:
Dragon gain Effective: Fell Dragon.
- **Lv 1**：Bond Forger
  - At the start of each turn, 
grants +2 to the Engage meter 
of all allies within 2 radius.
- **Lv 5**：Holy Shield
  - Halves stat-reduction effects, poison damage, 
and reduces damage from effectiveness.
- **Lv 10**：Bond Forger+
  - At the start of each turn, 
grants +3 to the Engage meter 
of all allies within 3 radius.
- **Lv 20**：Boon of Elyos
  - Grants the unit "Rally" command,
use to -4 Debuff to all allies in 2 radius.

## Engage Skills

- **Lv 1**：Attuned
  - If unit initiates combat and defeats a foe,
extends Engage duration by 1.
(Cannot exceed the maximum duration.)
