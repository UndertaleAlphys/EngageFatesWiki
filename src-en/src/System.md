# Calculator

- **Hit**  
  `Dex × 1.5 + Lck × 0.5 + Weapon Hit + Support Hit`

- **Avo**  
  `Spd × 1.5 + Lck × 0.5 + Weapon Avo + Support Avo + Terrain Avo`

- **Crit**  
  `(Dex - 4) × 0.5 + Weapon Crit + Support Crit`

- **Ddg (Dodge)**  
  `Lck × 0.5 + Weapon Ddg + Support Ddg`

- **Rod Hit**  
  `Mag + Dex + Weapon Hit`

- **Rod Avo**  
  `Res × 1.5 + Lck × 0.5 + Terrain Avo`

- **Chain Attack Damage**  
  `10% of foe’s Max HP`

- **Chain Attack Hit Rate**  
  `100`

- **Weapon Advantage**  
  `Unit grants +20 Hit and +20 Avo, deals +2 Dmg and takes -2 dmg if they have advantage of weapon`

---

# Follow-Up Mechanics

- **Follow-Up Coefficient**  
  By default, equal to the Speed difference between unit and foe.  
  (Some weapons may modify this value.)

- **Follow-Up Condition**  
  A unit can follow up if the coefficient is **5 or higher**.

---

# Engage Count

- If the unit **initiates combat** or **defeats an enemy**,  
  → `Engage Count +1`

- If the unit does **both** in one action,  
  → `Engage Count +2`

---

# SP System

- Units gain **SP = EXP**,  
  regardless of whether they equip an Emblem Ring.
